Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni pizza is made by layering a round, flattened dough base with a rich tomato sauce, followed by a generous sprinkle of shredded mozzarella cheese, and topping it with slices of spicy pepperoni.
The assembled pizza is baked in a hot oven until the crust turns golden, the cheese melts into a bubbly layer, and the pepperoni crisps slightly at the edges, creating a savory, flavorful classic.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Oregano
  • Black Pepper
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Shape the dough
  3. Add sauce and toppings
  4. Bake the pizza
  5. Serve and enjoy
