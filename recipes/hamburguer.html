Cheeseburguer

Grill a seasoned beef patty, toast the bun, then stack with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and your favorite condiments.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef Patty
  • Burguer Bun
  • Cheese Slice
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Form the patty
  2. Season the patty
  3. Cook the patty
  4. Toast the buns
  5. Assemble the burguer
